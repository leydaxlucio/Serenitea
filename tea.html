Chocolate Rose

black tea

Green Mango Peach

green tea

Green Mango Peach is ideal for people that like fruit-flavored green teas. Unsweetened or sweetened with honey, the flavor of mangoes and peaches provides an unusual blend of exotic flavors for lovers of green teas.

Flavor

tropical textured sweetness with a satisfying peppermint finish

Aroma

Orchid Vanilla

black tea

This smooth, medium-bodied, black tea is married with sweet vanilla from Madagascar, an island off the east coast of Africa known for producing the highest quality vanilla in the world.

Flavor

soft honey notes with a sweet, rounding smoothness from the vanilla

Aroma

sweet, tropical vanilla and nutty

Pumpkin Spice

black tea

Pumpkin Spice introduces a trio of classic fall spices – cinnamon, ginger, and clove – to organic black tea leaves, culminating in the perfect blend of autumnal flavors. Together, cinnamon, ginger, and clove are appreciated for their ability to transport our senses to a cabin in the woods, or a cafe on the corner as the seasons shift and golden leaves fall from above.

Flavor

spicy, pumpkin, chai spices

Aroma

zesty, ginger, clove
