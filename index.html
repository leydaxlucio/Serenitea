The Beginning

Tea parties have a long and illustrious history that dates back centuries, and they have evolved significantly over time. Tea has been enjoyed for centuries, and its popularity as a beverage spread throughout Europe in the 17th century.

The first recorded tea party in England was held in 1662 by Catherine of Braganza, the Portuguese wife of King Charles II.
